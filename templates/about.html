Company History

Beads and prints Africa was officially registered in December 2015 as an interior design company. Since then the company has grown to incorporate furniture leasing at a very pocket friendly price.

Mission statement

Beads and prints offers affordable, high quality furniture for office, home and events.

What we do

The company serves both corporate and personal needs, from leasing of office furniture, home and decor as well as sale of gently used furniture from those in our inventory that have ran their course

Our Strategy

Beads and prints aims at being the market leader in affordable furniture leasing; and the go-to for all thing space, planning and design.
